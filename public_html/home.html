ThumbDJ lets you...

  • Play music when you are away from the computer
  • Let your party pick the playlist
  • Text in songs to your friends' playlist

How to install...

Drag ThumbDJ bookmarklet to your bookmarks bar.

* Currently works with Firefox, Safari & Chrome

Start rocking in 3 steps!

Step 1

Drag the bookmarklet to your bookmark bar

Step 2

Go to Grooveshark and click the bookmarklet

Step 3

Follow the instructions and get the music on!
